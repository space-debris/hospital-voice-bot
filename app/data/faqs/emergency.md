# City General Hospital — Emergency Services

## Emergency Department
Our Emergency and Trauma Center operates **24 hours a day, 7 days a week**, including all holidays.

**Emergency Contact:** +91-11-2345-6700
**Ambulance Service:** 108 (government) or +91-11-2345-6701 (hospital ambulance)

## Location
The Emergency entrance is at the **rear of the hospital building** (South Gate). Follow signs for "Emergency / Casualty" from the main road.

## What to Bring
- Patient's ID (Aadhaar / any government-issued ID)
- Any existing medical records or prescriptions
- Insurance card (if applicable)
- List of current medications

## Emergency Services Available
- Trauma and accident care
- Chest pain evaluation / cardiac emergencies
- Stroke management
- Acute breathing difficulties
- Pediatric emergencies
- Poisoning / overdose management
- Fracture stabilization
- Minor surgical procedures

## Triage System
We follow a triage-based system:
1. **Red (Critical):** Immediate life-threatening — seen immediately
2. **Yellow (Urgent):** Serious but stable — seen within 15 minutes
3. **Green (Non-urgent):** Minor conditions — seen in order of arrival

## Ambulance Services
The hospital operates 3 fully equipped ambulances with paramedic staff.
- **Basic Life Support (BLS):** ₹1,500 within 10 km
- **Advanced Life Support (ALS):** ₹3,000 within 10 km
- Coverage area: Within 25 km of the hospital

## After Emergency Visit
- Patients requiring admission will be transferred to the appropriate ward.
- Patients discharged from emergency will receive a discharge summary and follow-up instructions.
- Please collect your emergency visit bill from the billing counter (Ground Floor) within 24 hours.
