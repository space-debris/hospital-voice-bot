## About the Hospital
City General Hospital (CGH) is a multi-specialty hospital located in the heart of the city, providing comprehensive healthcare services since 1995. The hospital is equipped with modern medical technology and staffed by experienced healthcare professionals.

## Address & Location
**Address:** 123, Health Avenue, Medical District, New Delhi - 110001

**Landmarks:** Near Central Metro Station, opposite City Park

**How to reach:**
- **By Metro:** Exit at Central Metro Station (Blue Line), take Gate 3. The hospital is a 5-minute walk.
- **By Bus:** Routes 301, 456, 789 stop at Medical District bus stand.
- **Parking:** Free parking available for patients. Multi-level parking at the rear entrance.

## Facilities
- 200-bed hospital with private, semi-private, and general wards
- 8 state-of-the-art operation theatres
- 24/7 Emergency and Trauma Center
- In-house pharmacy and diagnostic laboratory
- Cafeteria on the ground floor (7:00 AM - 9:00 PM)
- Free Wi-Fi in patient areas
- Wheelchair accessibility throughout the campus
- ATM available in the main lobby
