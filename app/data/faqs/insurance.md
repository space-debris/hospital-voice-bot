## Cashless Treatment Process
1. **Inform the hospital** at the time of admission that you wish to avail cashless facility.
2. **Submit documents:** Insurance card, policy number, ID proof, and any pre-authorization forms.
3. **Hospital TPA desk** (Ground Floor, Counter 5) will process the pre-authorization request.
4. **Approval:** Usually received within 2-4 hours.
5. **At discharge:** The approved amount is settled directly with the insurer. Patient pays only the non-covered amount (if any).

## Insurance Help Desk
- **Location:** Ground Floor, Counter 5

- **Phone:** +91-11-2345-6792
- **Documents needed:** Policy copy, insurance card, ID proof

## Important Notes
- Pre-authorization is required for planned/elective admissions. Please initiate at least 48 hours before admission.
- Emergency admissions: Pre-authorization will be processed within 24 hours of admission.
- Outpatient consultations and pharmacy bills are generally not covered under cashless (reimbursement basis only).
- For claim-related queries, please contact your insurance company directly or visit our TPA desk.
