## Important Notes
- OPD registration counters open 30 minutes before OPD start time.
- Last registration is accepted 30 minutes before closing.
- OPD is closed on Sundays and national holidays.
- Emergency services are available 24/7 regardless of OPD timings.
- Appointments are recommended to avoid long waiting times. Walk-in patients are accepted on a first-come, first-served basis.

## Appointment Booking
- **Online:** Through our website or by calling +91-11-2345-6790
- **At Hospital:** OPD registration counter, ground floor
- **Follow-up:** If you have a follow-up appointment, please carry your previous prescription and patient ID card.
