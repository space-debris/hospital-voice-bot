# City General Hospital — Visiting Hours & Patient Policies

## Visiting Hours
| Ward Type       | Morning         | Evening         |
|-----------------|-----------------|-----------------|
| General Ward    | 11:00 AM - 1:00 PM | 5:00 PM - 7:00 PM |
| Semi-Private    | 10:00 AM - 1:00 PM | 4:00 PM - 8:00 PM |
| Private Room    | 9:00 AM - 1:00 PM  | 4:00 PM - 9:00 PM |
| ICU / Critical  | 11:00 AM - 11:30 AM | 5:00 PM - 5:30 PM |

## Visitor Guidelines
- Maximum **2 visitors per patient** at a time.
- Children under 12 are **not allowed** in ICU or critical care areas.
- Visitors must collect a **visitor pass** from the reception desk.
- Please maintain **silence** in patient areas.
- **No outside food** is allowed in ICU/critical wards. General ward patients may receive home food with doctor's approval.
- Mobile phones must be on **silent mode** inside wards.
- **No photography or video recording** inside hospital premises without permission.

## Patient Admission Process
1. Visit the **Admission Counter** (Ground Floor) with doctor's referral/admission note.
2. Submit **ID proof** (Aadhaar/Passport/Voter ID) and **insurance documents** (if applicable).
3. Choose ward type (General / Semi-Private / Private).
4. Pay the **advance deposit** as applicable.
5. Collect your **patient ID wristband** and room/bed assignment.

## Discharge Process
1. Doctor issues **discharge summary** and clearance.
2. Patient/attendant visits **Billing Counter** (Ground Floor) for final bill settlement.
3. Collect **discharge medicines** from the pharmacy (with prescription).
4. Return the **patient ID wristband** at the discharge counter.
5. A **follow-up appointment** will be scheduled as recommended by the doctor.

## Patient Rights
- Right to receive respectful, quality medical care
- Right to complete information about diagnosis and treatment
- Right to privacy and confidentiality
- Right to a second medical opinion
- Right to refuse treatment (with informed consent documentation)
- Right to access medical records
